About Chatbot App

Your personal assistant, redefined.

Project Overview

Chatbot App is a web-based project designed to provide assistance to users by answering questions, generating ideas, and more.

Key Features

  • Seamless login with traditional methods and third-party providers.
  • Ability to start a fresh conversation while preserving session security.
  • Strong password hashing and secure login management.
  • Logout option to ensure account safety.

Why This Project?

This project was created as part of my journey to join the 'CSEC_ASTU DEV' division, showcasing my skills in web development and chatbot design.

Future Goals

My vision is to enhance the chatbot's capabilities by enabling it to remember past conversations and provide more concise and tailored information to users.

Developed independently with passion and dedication.
